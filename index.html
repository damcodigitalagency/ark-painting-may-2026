Section 03
Observations, Analysis & Next Steps
Observations & Analysis
What the Data Is Telling Us
  • 1 The Video Ad — Spring Offer + Reviews was the only creative to produce a confirmed booking. One lead, one appointment. This confirms that pairing real customer testimonials with a specific, time-bound offer gives homeowners the confidence to commit — not just browse.
  • 2 The Spring Video Ad generated 4 leads — the most in May — but zero bookings. The CRM tells the full story: one lead was outside the service area, one explicitly said "just looking," and the remaining two had no meaningful outcome. Lead volume without intent is not a win.
  • 3 Carousel 3 — Exterior generated one lead who "booked someone else." That is a qualified buyer with active intent and real budget who chose a competitor — not because the ad failed, but because follow-up speed did. This creative is attracting the right audience.
  • 4 The Waterford out-of-area lead triggered a full targeting review — resulting in 150+ pin codes being excluded from the campaign. This is the most impactful optimisation made in May. Better targeting directly reduces wasted spend without changing the budget.
Next Steps
What Is Planned for June
  • 1 New creative refresh. Fresh ad concepts are being developed to test new visual angles and extend campaign reach beyond the current creative set.
  • 2 Call Ads campaign launch. A dedicated click-to-call campaign will be launched to increase lead volume and remove the form-fill friction for homeowners who are ready to speak directly.
  • 3 Two-email automation sequence deployed on every form submission going forward:
    📧 Email 1: Instant auto-reply on form fill — "We'll contact you within 24 hours." 📧 Email 2: Follow-up to non-responders with the spring offer and direct contact number — 586-473-7050.
Action Required from ArK Painting
What the Team Needs to Do
  • 1 Share the existing customer list. A list of past ArK Painting customers will allow Damco Digital to build a Lookalike Audience — targeting new homeowners who closely match the profile of people who have already hired ArK. This is one of the strongest targeting improvements available.
  • 2 Contact every new lead within 24 hours. Homeowners who fill out a form are actively comparing contractors at that moment. A lead not called within 24 hours is a lead that has likely already moved on. Fast follow-up is the single highest-impact action ArK Painting can take to increase the booking rate from the same ad spend.
  • 3 Add call remarks when following up. Currently the team logs only the outcome status (Scheduled, Called, Spam). To improve lead quality and targeting over time, please also note: Was the person genuinely interested? Were they available to speak? Was it a spam or irrelevant enquiry? What was the reason they didn't book? These remarks help Damco Digital identify marketing-qualified leads and optimise the campaign accordingly.
